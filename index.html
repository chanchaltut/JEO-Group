A Legacy of Innovation, A Future of Possibilities

With a strong foundation built in 1987, JEO Group has emerged as one of India's leading multifaceted business conglomerates. We are driven by a vision to shape infrastructure, energize communities, and engineer solutions that uplift industries and lives alike.

Spanning a dynamic range of industries, JEO Group is a pioneer in the manufacturing of high-performance Distribution and Power Transformers, supported by large-scale fabrication facilities. As turnkey contractors for both Electrical and Civil works, we manage complex infrastructure projects from concept to completion with unmatched technical expertise and project management precision.

Our Areas of Expertise

JEO Group brings together years of experience, technical mastery, and a commitment to excellence across multiple domains in the power and infrastructure sectors. Our capabilities span from manufacturing to turnkey project execution, making us a one-stop solution for high-quality, reliable, and scalable energy infrastructure.

Plant Design & Engineering

Our in-house engineering team provides holistic plant layout planning, design optimization, and electrical system engineering. We ensure that every plant we help build operates efficiently, safely, and sustainably from day one.

Learn More

Solar Power Solutions

Our solar division designs, installs, and maintains solar energy systems for residential, commercial, and industrial clients. From rooftop setups to ground-mounted solutions, we empower you to transition to clean and renewable energy with confidence.

Learn More

Power & Distribution Transformer Manufacturing

We specialize in the design and manufacturing of robust, high-performance power and distribution transformers. Our units are built to meet industry standards and customized for diverse voltage applications across utilities, commercial zones, and industrial plants.

Learn More

Fabrication of HT/LT Line Materials

Our advanced fabrication unit produces a wide range of transmission and distribution line materials including poles, clamps, brackets, cross-arms, and hardware. These components are essential for building durable, efficient, and safe HT/LT electrical infrastructure.

Learn More

A-Class Electrical Contracting

As a licensed A-Class Electrical Contractor, we execute turnkey electrical projects across rural and urban India. From substation installations and overhead line erections to complete electrification solutions, we handle every stage — from planning to commissioning — with professionalism and precision.

Learn More

Operations & Maintenance (O&M)

We offer end-to-end O&M services tailored to ensure the smooth functioning, longevity, and safety of your electrical systems and power plants. Our preventive and corrective maintenance solutions reduce downtime and maximize asset performance.
