Our Journey Since 1987

Spanning a dynamic range of industries, JEO Group is a pioneer in the manufacturing of high-performance Distribution and Power Transformers, supported by large-scale fabrication facilities. As turnkey contractors for both Electrical and Civil works, we manage complex infrastructure projects from concept to completion with unmatched technical expertise and project management precision.

Our growing portfolio also includes the design, supply, and installation of solar panel systems, offering clean and sustainable energy solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial use. We are a trusted supplier of LT & HT Transmission and Distribution Line Materials, ensuring reliable performance across all our projects.

Backed by a commitment to innovation, safety, and sustainability, JEO Group continues to evolve — building smarter, stronger, and more connected solutions for tomorrow's India.
